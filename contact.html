Mission

Driven by a commitment to empower and uplift Cipher Educational Consult Limited seeks to bridge the educational divide, particularly for those affected by insurgency in northeastern Nigeria. Our founders, intimately acquainted with the challenges, aspire to showcase the resilience and talent of the region to the world. At the heart of our mission is the Educational Sponsorship program deserving, designed to empower deserving students from northeastern Nigeria.
